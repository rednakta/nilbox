## When do I need a commercial license?

You likely need a commercial license if you want to:

- Bundle or embed nilbox (or a derivative) into a **proprietary / closed-source** product.
- Distribute a modified nilbox **without** releasing your modifications under the GPL.
- Obtain terms that GPL-3.0 does not provide (e.g. warranty, indemnification, priority
  support, or a written license agreement for procurement/compliance).

You do **not** need a commercial license to:

- Use nilbox internally within your organization.
- Evaluate, test, or develop with nilbox.
- Contribute to nilbox, or build and distribute it under the GPL-3.0.
